Pixelated_Lagg

Pixelated_Lagg is a teenage developer who specializes in creating applications and 2d/3d game environments. They are fluent in c# and somewhat competent in html/css. Having created Headshot Studios in April of 2021, Pixelated_Lagg is always working on a new game or piece of software.

Jump Squared

Jump Squared is a basic 2d platformer based on Geometry Dash and Jump King. It is centered around a nameless and emotionless blue square who needs to get to the white platform at the end of each level. Currently, there are 3 levels in game, and I am working to finish the game with a 4th level.
